Her Story

Lisa graduated in 1977 from the University of Connecticut with a BFA in Theater, concentrating in directing and dance. She spent two years in Los Angeles studying film acting, while filling her days with dance classes, the gym, the pool, running, and biking.

She moved back east and spent two years working at a Gloria Stevens salon in Newport, Rhode Island, before moving to Martha's Vineyard in 1982. In 1984, she became AFAA (Aerobics and Fitness Association of America) certified and began a part-time career as a fitness instructor.

She married, built her studio, and raised two children, later upgrading the space into the friendly, beautiful studio it is today.

"Turning People On To Fitness" is what I enjoy doing, and hope to do it for years to come!

Mission

Her Mission

I believe that fostering and maintaining our physical health sets the foundation for a vibrant, balanced and meaningful life. It is my mission to work safely and smartly with my clients to help them discover their unique potential for strength, flexibility and vitality. I am passionate about fitness, and it is my mission to do this amazing and rewarding work for years to come.
