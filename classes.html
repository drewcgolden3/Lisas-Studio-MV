What a Class Includes

Every class is a complete, non-competitive workout, no machines, no repetitive boring motions.

  • 01 Flexibility warm-up to get the body ready.
  • 02 Strength training with a variety of props: free weights, tubing, bands, and more.
  • 03 A 25-minute cardio dance segment set to a variety of music.
  • 04 Mat work for core and abdominal strength.
  • 05 Ballet barre for hip mobility and leg exercises.
