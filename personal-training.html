Personal Training

We'll Build Your Goals Together

Personal training with Lisa isn't about being told what to do. It's a partnership, guided by four decades of teaching and real anatomical knowledge.

Her Approach

"My approach to personal training is simple. We will create clear goals together. People come to me thinking I'm going to tell them what to do. But, I'm not. We're going to work together. I will help you in any way that you deem necessary, along with my guidance and suggestions."

Lisa Amols

The success of a personal trainer isn't measured by how long they retain a client, but by how much the client learns from her. That's the philosophy AFAA instilled in Lisa from the start: teachers first and foremost.

What to Expect

What a Session Looks Like

  • — Lisa sets up the room specifically for you, building on what you worked on last time.
  • — She keeps a record of your sessions and uses it to plan the next one, so you always feel a real sense of progress.
  • — No two sessions are the same. Expect free weights, tubing, bands, mats, and ballet barre work, tailored to your goals.
  • — Lisa explains the anatomy behind each exercise, so you understand why you're doing it, not just how.
