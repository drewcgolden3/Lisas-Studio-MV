Meet Lisa

Turning People On To Fitness

Lisa Amols has been teaching fitness on Martha's Vineyard since 1982. AFAA certified since 1984, she built her studio from the ground up into the friendly, beautiful space it is today, and has spent over four decades helping islanders of every age and ability get stronger.

"My approach to personal training is simple. We will create clear goals together. People come to me thinking I'm going to tell them what to do. But I'm not. We're going to work together."
